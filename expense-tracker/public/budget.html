💡 Budgeting Tips

50/30/20 Rule

50% needs, 30% wants, 20% savings. A simple framework for healthy finances.

Track Every Rupee

Log every expense, no matter how small. Small purchases add up quickly over time.

Review Monthly

Check your budget at month end. Adjust limits based on your actual spending patterns.

Emergency Fund

Aim for 3–6 months of expenses saved. It provides a financial safety net.
